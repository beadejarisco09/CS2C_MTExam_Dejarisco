Calle Crisologo

Calle Crisologo is a historic cobblestone street in Vigan, Ilocos Sur, famous for its well-preserved Spanish colonial houses, horse-drawn carriages (kalesa), and vibrant shops.

Awa-sen Falls

Awa-sen Falls in Ilocos Sur is a hidden gem surrounded by lush greenery, perfect for relaxation and swimming in its cool, clear waters.

Santa Lucia Chruch

Santa Lucia Church in Ilocos Sur is a centuries-old Spanish-era church known for its classic architecture and religious heritage. It’s a peaceful place for prayer and reflection.
